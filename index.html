E-Plus for Educators

An application that makes it easy to grade assignments from third party tools like Quizlet and custom assignments while walking around the classroom with your smart phone.

Technology is pushing forward, but a majority of technological benefits come at the cost of obscurity. Online services offer to teach and grade simple assignments (like vocabulary) with less indimidation, while increasing the probability of retention and repetition.

Access to the automated grading tools are often masked behind APIs, making enthusiastic teacher's work harder by grading manually, or taking time out of their lives learning the interfaces.

Homework assignments are usually tedious to grade, and many teachers, if given the appropriate tools, could simply walk around the classroom and visually grade student assignments. This application aims to make this process seamless.

In the future, I hope to implement gamification-based tools to attempt to motivate students, while giving them a birds-eye view of their progress.

FAQ

How much will this cost?
At this point, I am not considering a price. I want to release the application for free. Any IT or tech-savvy teacher at a school could then set up the application on an inexpensive server for themselves or another teacher.
When can I have it?
If I have two days a week, I could finish the initial quizlet-tied application for grading assignments in two weeks. If I wanted to implement the gamification-based application, I would need about 6 months depending on the technical difficulty and modularity I will build it with.
